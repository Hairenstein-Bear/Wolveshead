Wolveshead was opened in March of 2015 by John and Jackie Breiner. Thanks to support from the local community, and a wonderful staff, Wolveshead became a staple of Lakewood Ranch in no time.

Now we continue the formula that made us successful: Great food, Great Atmosphere, and Fair prices. Our menu consists of delicious, made from scratch items including signature pizza and wings. With 22 beers on tap, as well as a large wine, seltzer and bottled beer selection we have something for everyone.

We have MLB Extra Innings, NFL Sunday ticket, and all Tampa Bay Lightning games displayed on our 15 T.Vs. Come find out why we are a regular part of so many peoples week, and JOIN THE PACK!
